Get to Know Us

Our vision is to create a better society through the transformation of orphan and vulnerable children’s lives

We seek to do so by providing them with life skills (through education, and moral and Christian teachings) and by teaching them how to live out their civil rights and responsibilities, so that they become symbols of hope and change in their communities once reintegrated.

Our Vision

To raise orphan and vulnerable children up to become better adults – those whose future families will be influenced by and educated in the values that they learnt during their time with us.

To create a better society through the transformation of orphan and vulnerable children’s lives. We seek to do so by providing them with life skills (through education, and moral and Christian teachings) and by teaching them how to live out their civil rights and responsibilities, so that they become symbols of hope and change in their communities once reintegrated.
