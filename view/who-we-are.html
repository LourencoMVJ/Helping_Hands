Who We Are?

At Helping Hands, we are a dedicated team committed to improving the lives of underprivileged children in Mozambique — providing education, healthcare, and nutrition to those who need it most. Every child deserves the chance to thrive, regardless of their circumstances.

What began as a simple idea — to make a meaningful impact through community support and compassion — has grown into a network of volunteers, donors and partners working together for lasting change. We believe true change comes from collective, community-driven action, working hand-in-hand with local leaders to ensure our impact is sustainable and transparent.
