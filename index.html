Get to know us

Our vision is to create a better society through the transformation of orphan and vulnerable children's lives.

We seek to do so by providing them with life skills — through education, and moral and Christian teachings — and by teaching them how to live out their civil rights and responsibilities, so that they become symbols of hope and change in their communities once reintegrated.
